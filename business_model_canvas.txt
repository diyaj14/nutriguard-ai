STRATEGIC BUSINESS PILLARS
==========================

1. The "Safety First" Competitive Moat
--------------------------------------
Most apps (MyFitnessPal, etc.) focus on calories. NutriGuard focus on safety and suitability. By flagging MSG, allergens, and salt-ratios for hypertensive patients, we create a specialized tool that users depend on for their health, not just their weight.

2. The Recommendation Flywheel
------------------------------
The "Better Alternative" Suggestion Engine is the engine of growth.
- User scans a 3/10 score biscuit.
- App suggests a 9/10 score millet cookie.
- The millet cookie brand pays a small affiliate commission or "sponsored placement" fee.
- This provides value to the user while generating non-intrusive revenue.

3. Data-as-a-Service (Preventative Care)
----------------------------------------
Anonymized trend data on what people are scanning can be incredibly valuable for public health researchers and food manufacturers looking to develop healthier products for specific demographics (e.g., "What are diabetic users in Mumbai scanning most?").

4. Expansion into B2B
---------------------
NutriGuard for Hospitals/Clinics: Doctors can "prescribe" NutriGuard to their patients to monitor their adherence to a prescribed diet, using the History + Trend Dashboard as the reporting interface.
