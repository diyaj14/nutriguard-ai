## âš ï¸ **Troubleshooting**

### **"Camera permission denied"**
**Solution:**
1. Click the camera icon in your browser's address bar
2. Change camera permission to "Allow"
3. Refresh the page

### **"No cameras found"**
**Solution:**
- Make sure your device has a camera
- Try a different browser (Chrome works best)
- Check if another app is using the camera

### **"Scanner not detecting barcode"**
**Solution:**
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold camera steady
- Move closer/farther from barcode
- Make sure barcode is clear and not damaged

### **Camera is blurry**
**Solution:**
- Clean your camera lens
- Tap on the screen to focus (on some devices)
- Try switching to the other camera
